✓ Do

  • Keep clear space = the badge's inner-disc radius.
  • Use the badge on any colour — it carries its own dark disc.
  • Let cyan land as a single accent.

✕ Don't

  • Recolour the W gradient or the white S.
  • Remove or add beacon dots; stretch, skew or rotate.
  • Introduce a second accent colour (no orange).
